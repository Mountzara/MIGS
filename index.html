About Dr. Zara

Dr. Zara is a board-certified OB/GYN specializing in Minimally Invasive Gynecologic Surgery (MIGS). With expertise in advanced laparoscopic and robotic techniques, she provides compassionate care focused on minimizing recovery time and maximizing patient outcomes.

Trained at leading institutions and staying current with the latest surgical innovations, Dr. Zara is committed to finding the right treatment approach for each individual patient.

Dr. Zara is dedicated to patient education, ensuring that every woman understands her condition and treatment options to make informed decisions about her health.
