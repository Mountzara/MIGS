About Me

Gynecologist specializing in Minimally Invasive Gynecologic Surgery (MIGS) with extensive experience in advanced laparoscopy, hysteroscopy, and robotic surgical techniques. Dedicated to providing patient-centered care and advancing the field through research, education, and innovation.

As a MIGS specialist, I focus on treating complex gynecologic conditions using minimally invasive approaches that reduce recovery time, minimize complications, and improve patient outcomes. My clinical interests include endometriosis, fibroids, pelvic pain, and complex benign gynecologic pathology.
